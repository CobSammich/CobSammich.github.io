Update (8/13/22) After Information

So after reading some of the reviews of classes on OMSCentral, there are a few take-aways:

  • ML4T teaches outdated information - Maybe good to wait for the course to be revamped before taking
  • CP is poorly run and has no community to get involved with classmates.
  • AI is a good class for learning algorithms - good first semester course.
  • GIOS is a good class to sharpen C/C++ skills and learn about Operating Systems - Unpopular, also a good class.
